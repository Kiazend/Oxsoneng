Specialists in high quality new builds, renovations and conservation in Devon and Cornwall

Bovey Construction started 32 years ago as a traditional family run construction company. Today, we are proud to be a team of over 30 skilled construction professionals and a CIOB Chartered Building Company. We specialise in high quality, complex construction projects across Devon and Cornwall with a portfolio that includes new houses, major refurbishments and historic works.

Residential

Working closely with architects and designers delivering highly individual, complex, new build and refurbishment projects. Our team have the knowledge to bring the highest specifications and most technically challenging designs to life.

Historic

Highly experienced in working on listed buildings and in the conservation of buildings of historic significance. We understand the importance of craftsmanship, material selection and compliance with heritage organisations.

Find Out More

Commercial

We act as main contractor for developers, local authorities and communities designing buildings sustainably and responsibly. We have the ability to build to Passivhaus or any other standard and are familiar with the principles of building biology, climate change, ease of occupier operation and whole life costing.

Find Out More

With our experience of a wide variety of approaches to client and contractor engagement we are able to be adaptable to non-traditional approaches to realise best value for your project.

Bovey Concept encompasses a flexible approach to client engagement to best suit the needs of the individual project, client and stage of design.

From fee based management arrangements, negotiated approaches to enable early engagement, or a flexible hybrid of more traditional forms, we are always happy to listen and discuss how we can help make your project a reality and through our experience add value to the process. Be it first steps starting from scratch or from an already developed design.
